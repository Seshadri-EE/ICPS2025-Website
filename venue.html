About the Conference Venue: Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH)

The Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH), established in 2008, is recognized for its commitment to teaching, research, and innovation. Consistently ranked among the top engineering schools in India by NIRF, IITH offers an academic environment where research and education come together to address contemporary challenges. The institute encourages interdisciplinary collaboration, providing students with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.

IITH’s campus is equipped with modern facilities and has developed meaningful partnerships with industry and academic institutions around the world, supporting advancements in various technological areas.

We are delighted to host ICPS 2025 at IITH and invite you to discover the campus. The Department of Electrical Engineering (EE) is central to the institute’s academic and research initiatives, offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The department is engaged in cutting-edge research across several specialized fields, including Power Systems, Power Electronics, Smart Grids, Microelectronics, VLSI, Communications, Signal Processing, as well as emerging areas like Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).

We look forward to welcoming you to IITH for ICPS 2025 and invite you to explore the campus, where you can experience the vibrant academic atmosphere that supports innovation and practical approaches in electrical engineering.

How to Reach IIT Hyderabad

Below are the detailed directions to help you reach the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH) from various key locations in the city. Choose the most convenient mode of transport for your journey.

From Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (64 km)

If you are arriving by air, follow these directions:

  • Take the Airport Approach Road and head towards Gandiguda to join the Outer Ring Road (ORR).
  • Continue on the ORR, heading towards Muthangi. Take exit 3 from the ORR.
  • Drive along Mumbai Highway (NH 9) for approximately 15 km. The IITH signboard will appear on your right-hand side.

From Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station (54 km)

If you're arriving by bus, follow these directions:

  • Head north towards Mumbai Highway (NH 65).
  • Proceed via Kukatpally and Patancheruvu, following the ORR towards Mumbai Highway (NH 65).
  • Take the exit for Mumbai Highway (NH 65) and continue for 15 km.
  • The IITH signboard will be visible on your right-hand side.

From Secunderabad Railway Station (50 km)

If you are arriving by train, follow these directions:

  • Take SD Road to Mahatma Gandhi Road (NH 7) in Hyderabad.
  • Follow NH 7 to Old Airport Road, passing through New Bowenpally.
  • Continue on Old Airport Road and head towards Bhavani Nagar.
  • Drive to Mumbai Highway (NH 65) and continue for 15 km. The IITH signboard will be on your right side.

From Hitech City (38 km)

If you are traveling from Hitech City, follow these directions:

  • Take Gachibowli – Miyapur Road to NH65 in Hafeezpet (approximately 19 minutes, 6.9 km).
  • Drive to NH65 in Kandi, and continue for about 15 km until you see the IITH signboard on your right side.

Metro Route

If you prefer to take the metro, follow these steps:

  • Start from the Secunderabad-East Metro Station (approximately 600 meters from Secunderabad Railway Station).
  • Purchase a ticket to Miyapur Station, but note that you will need to change metros at Ameerpet Station.
  • At Ameerpet, board a metro towards Miyapur Station.
  • From Miyapur Station, take a bus to Patancheru Bus Stop.
  • Finally, take a bus to Sangareddy Town, where you will find the IITH signboard on your right-hand side.

MMTS Route

If you're using the MMTS suburban train service, here’s how to proceed:

  • Take an MMTS train from Secunderabad Railway Station to Lingampally Railway Station.
  • From Lingampally Railway Station, take a bus to Lingampally Bus Stand.
  • From there, take a bus to Patancheru Bus Stand, and then another bus to Sangareddy Town.
  • The IITH signboard will be visible on your right-hand side.
