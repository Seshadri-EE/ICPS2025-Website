About ICPS 2025

The International Conference on Power Systems (ICPS) is a leading biennial forum established in 2004, dedicated to advancing the field of Power Engineering. It serves as a platform for experts from academia, industry, research, and practice to exchange knowledge, discuss emerging trends, and explore innovative solutions across various power system domains. The 11th edition of ICPS will be held from December 7-9, 2025, at IIT Hyderabad, India. This conference aims to foster global collaboration in the power systems domain, focusing on key areas such as renewable energy integration, smart grid innovations, and AI-based solutions for enhanced grid management. ICPS continues its tradition of exploring the latest advancements and innovations in power systems to address the challenges of the future.

Organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Hyderabad.

Key Themes of ICPS 2025

ICPS 2025 will feature various important themes in power systems, such as:

  • Smart Generation, Transmission & Distribution
  • Power Electronics & Smart Grid Control
  • Grid Integration of Renewable Energy
  • Machine Learning & Data Analytics in Power Systems
  • Energy Storage & Grid Optimization
  • Cybersecurity in Power Systems
