Camera-Ready Paper Submission Guidelines

Instructions for Submission of Camera Ready Paper

To ensure that accepted papers are published in the conference proceedings and considered for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, authors are required to complete the following steps. The steps below provide an overview of the process; detailed instructions for each step follow afterward.

Process Outline for Final Paper Submission

  • Final Camera-Ready Paper Preparation: Prepare the final version of your paper according to the prescribed formatting guidelines. Make sure that the reviewer comments are addressed in the camera-ready version.
  • Inclusion of IEEE Copyright Notice: Insert the official IEEE copyright notice in the footer of the first page of your paper.
  • PDF eXpress Compliance Check: Validate your paper using IEEE PDF eXpress to confirm compatibility with IEEE Xplore requirements.
  • Camera-Ready Paper Submission: Submit the PDF eXpress–verified camera-ready version of your paper through the submission portal.
  • Copyright Form Submission: Complete and submit the IEEE Electronic Copyright Form (eCF).
  • Author Registration: Ensure that at least one author registers for the conference with a full registration.
  • Conference Presentation: At least one of the authors must present the paper at the conference. Papers not presented will not be forwarded for IEEE Xplore.

Detailed Instructions

Step 1: Final Camera-Ready Paper Preparation

  • Paper title and author order must remain unchanged in the final submission.
  • Papers must adhere to the IEEE two-column format. Please follow the IEEE two-column A4 size standard conference format. Download the IEEE template here.
  • Revise your paper based on reviewers comments (available on the conference Microsoft CMT site).
  • Papers should not exceed six (6) A4 pages, including references.
  • The similarity index must be less than 15% (excluding references). See the IEEE Plagiarism Policy for details.
  • Submit papers as a PDF file (max size: 3 MB).

Step 2: Inclusion of IEEE Copyright Notice

Add the appropriate IEEE copyright notice to the bottom of the first page (leftmost column) of your paper. If you are unsure which notice to use, please contact Dr. Rupesh Wandhare.

  • U.S. Government Authors: U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright
  • Crown Government (UK, Canada, Australia): 979-8-3315-5599-3/25/$31.00 ©2025 Crown
  • European Union Authors: 979-8-3315-5599-3/25/$31.00 ©2025 European Union
  • All Others: 979-8-3315-5599-3/25/$31.00 ©2025 IEEE

Step 3: PDF eXpress Check

  • Ensure your source document is final and requires no further revisions.
  • Log in to the IEEE PDF eXpress site. To create a PDF eXpress account, follow these steps:
  • For First-Time Users:
    • Click on “New Users - Click Here”.
    • Enter the following:
      • Conference ID: 67276X
      • Your email address
      • A password of your choice
    • Continue entering information as prompted.
    • You will see a confirmation online and receive an email verifying your account setup.
  • For Returning Users:
    • Use the same email and password as previous conferences.
    • Verify that your contact information is up to date.

Step 4: Camera-Ready Paper Submission

After your paper has successfully passed the IEEE PDF eXpress™ compliance check, submit the final camera-ready version through the Author Console in CMT. Before submitting, please ensure that:

  • Rename the PDF eXpress–verified file to PIDxxx (where xxx is your paper ID in CMT) before uploading.
  • The paper title must remain unchanged from the original submission.
  • The author list and order must remain the same as the original submission.

Step 5: Submission of the Copyright Form

Once the camera-ready paper is uploaded, complete the copyright transfer:

  1. In CMT, click on “Submit IEEE Copyright Form”.
  2. Follow the link to the IEEE eCF website.
  3. Complete and submit the IEEE Electronic Copyright Form.
  4. Download the completed form and upload it into CMT.

Note: The corresponding author may submit the copyright form on behalf of all authors.

Step 6: Author Registration

At least one author of each accepted paper must register with a full registration. Registration details are available at: https://icps2025.in/registration.html

Step 7: Paper Presentation at the Conference

Each accepted paper must be presented at the conference to be included in the proceedings and submitted to IEEE Xplore®. Detailed instructions for preparing presentations will be published on the conference website soon.
