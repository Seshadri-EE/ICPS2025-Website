Conference Program

Program Overview

The ICPS 2025 program is designed to bring together experts, researchers, and practitioners across the power systems community. Over three days, participants will experience a blend of tutorials, keynote lectures, technical paper presentations, industry interactions, and cultural events.

Important: The schedule below is tentative and is subject to revisions. Detailed session-level information — including presentation timings, room allocations, and speaker lists — will be updated on this page very soon.
