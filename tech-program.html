Technical Program — ICPS 2025

Program at a glance

The ICPS 2025 Technical Program spans over three-day program and features a structured blend of tutorials, keynote lectures, parallel paper sessions, poster presentations, and thematic discussions spanning multiple tracks within power systems, power electronics, optimization, data analytics, and emerging technologies.

Note: This is the tentative technical program. Room/venue allocation and final timing updates will follow.
