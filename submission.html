ICPS 2025 Paper Submission Guidelines

Author and Paper Submission Guidelines

The following guidelines must be strictly adhered to while submitting your manuscript to ICPS 2025:

  • Language: All submissions must be in English.
  • Originality: Papers must be original and not under consideration for publication elsewhere.
  • Format: Papers must adhere to the IEEE two-column format. They should not exceed six (6) A4 pages in length. Submit papers as a PDF file (max. size: 2MB). Download the IEEE template here.
  • Plagiarism: All papers will undergo a plagiarism check. Papers with a similarity index of 15% or higher (excluding references) may be rejected without review. Check IEEE Plagiarism Policy for more details.
  • Submission Portal: Only submissions made through the CMT Portal will be accepted.
  • Track Selection: Choose the track that best matches the content of your paper for appropriate session scheduling and reviewer assignment. The list of tracks is available here.
  • Review Process: All submitted papers will undergo a single-blind review process. This means that the identities of the authors will be known to the reviewers, but the authors will not know the identities of the reviewers. Reviewers' comments must be addressed in the final version of the paper.
  • Final Submission: Paper title and author order must remain unchanged in the final submission.

Submission Portal Details

Important Notice: It is proposed to use the CMT portal for paper submissions. The approval from CMT is currently pending and will be updated here once finalized. Authors are requested to follow the CMT link for submissions, adhering to the conference timeline once approval is granted.

Acknowledgement: The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-reviewing process for this conference. This service was provided for free by Microsoft and they bore all expenses, including costs for Azure cloud services as well as for software development and support.

Steps for Submitting Your Paper

  1. Log in to the CMT Submission Portal.
  2. Select the track that best matches your paper.
  3. Complete the submission form with your details.
  4. Upload your paper in PDF format.
