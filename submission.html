Author and Manuscript Guidelines

The following are the detailed guidelines for the submission of your manuscript to the conference. Kindly review them thoroughly prior to submitting your paper.

  • All submissions must be in English.
  • Papers must be original and not under consideration for publication elsewhere.
  • Papers must be formatted strictly according to IEEE two-column format. They should not exceed six (6) A4 pages in length and must be submitted as a PDF file (maximum size: 2MB) [The template can be downloaded from here] .
  • The conference adheres to a strict plagiarism policy. Authors are encouraged to check for plagiarism before submitting their papers, and plagiarism checks must be done prior to uploading to IEEE Xplore. For more details, refer to IEEE Plagiarism Policy.
  • Only submissions made through CMT will be considered. Papers submitted via other means will not be accepted.
  • Submissions must be in PDF format. No other formats will be accepted.
  • All submitted papers will undergo a plagiarism check. Papers with a similarity index of 15% or higher (excluding references) may be rejected without review.
  • During submission, authors should select one topic category that best aligns with the content of their paper. This helps the committee assign appropriate reviewers and schedule sessions.
  • If your paper fits the conference theme but does not match any listed topics, please contact the Technical Program Chair at chair@icps2025.in for guidance.
  • A unique Paper ID will be assigned to each submission. Please reference this ID in all communications.
  • Authors will be notified of acceptance or rejection via email by the date specified on the homepage. If revisions are required, you will be informed. Final camera-ready papers must be submitted by the specified deadline.
  • Paper title and author order must remain unchanged in the final submission.
  • Reviewers' comments must be addressed in the final paper before submission.
